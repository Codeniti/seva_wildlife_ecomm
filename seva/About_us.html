SAVE TIGER

SEVA DEVOTED TO SAVING TIGERS,OUR NATURAL WORLD AND OUR ONE ONLY,MOTHER EARTH.


This journey with SEVA began two years ago, when I met Phil Davis, author of ENCOUNTERS WITH TIGERS IN INDIA.I was horrified to learn about how our tigers and leopards are being brutally killed and being sold for their so called mystical powers, which scientists have proved not true! Our forests are being cut down for more development, roads, highways to pass through, for shopping malls, more hotels and resorts to be built . Coal mines are more important to generate more money.Really? I thought to myself.Whats important to us? We all want development, but at what expense, and Whose expense? When and where do we stop? Do we have go on until the last tree is cut, the last fish caught, the last tiger killed? What are the repercussions? There will be no clean air and water if we carry on this way. Tigers and leopards are top carnivores and apex animals in our forests. We would sustain the food chain as all life forms are interlinked with each other. Hence, conserving wilderness is crucial for mans own survival. We must respect all life forms on Earth so that we can save our precious Mother Earth. Living in cities, we all seem to feel so disconnected with our wilderness, our natural world, that we dont realize the devastation thats happening .We feel helpless. how and what can we do? Well, this is where we step in! We want to act as a bridge, for people who live in cities, who cant practically help inside forests. I thought of a t-shirt line, SEVA. As we can raise awareness amongst people in India and abroad through messages and art-form on the t-shirts. Also, by involving celebrities to wear SEVA tees, we can promote this cause to gain attention through help of media.


With our efforts and support of the general public, we hope to bring changes for our Mother Earth, as her soil is the one and only that we walk on and build our dreams on.


This is what the brand Seva advocates. It is a brand ,first in its kind to create awareness in art form and messages ont-shirts in 100%- cotton. The designer, Harish Parekh, is from London, England. He has designed these t-shirts keeping in mind a social responsibility to try and help our environment.


We as a brand are in association with N.G.Os for SAVING TIGERS, we will be donating from sales of t-shirts to buy necessary equipments such as trap cameras, rescue- cars, motor bikes, etc to help on the ground level, for the N.G.Os who are trying so hard in the forests..


Usp of the t-shirts is that we are spreading awareness in art form,to help our environment,thus our planet. This what makes SEVA unique above all lovable.
